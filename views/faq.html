FAQ

What is this Site?

This site is a music streaming service that allows users to listen to music for free.

How do I listen to music?

To listen to music, simply click on the song you want to listen to and it will start playing.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the login button in the top right corner of the page and then click on the sign up button.

How do I log in?

To log in, click on the login button in the top right corner of the page and then enter your username and password.
